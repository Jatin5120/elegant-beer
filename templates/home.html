About the Place

The ELEGANT BEER BAR started as a craft beer store founded by Riya and Karan Mehta in 2013. We recently opened our taproom expansion in 2017, to offer a unique selection of draft beers from around the world and share our passion with our patrons. Providing beer education is a core mission of The ELEGANT BEER BAR.
